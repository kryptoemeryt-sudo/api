HOW IT WORKS
01
MINT A FIGURE
Pay in CHZ and receive a unique generative football player. Country, position, rarity and accessories are all encoded in DNA on-chain. 95% of every mint goes to the prize vault. Each figure is a unique NFT — no two are alike.
02
TRADE ON MARKET
All figures are tradeable on our built-in marketplace. Got a weak figure? Sell it. Want a country you believe in? Buy it. Prices rise as teams advance — a France figure is worth more after every win. 5% royalty goes back to the prize pool.
03
WATCH THE WORLD CUP
Every match matters. Your score = figure weight × country multiplier:

🏆 Champion × 5.0
🥈 Finalist × 3.0
Semi-final × 2.0
Quarter-final × 1.5
Round of 16 × 1.2
Round of 32 × 1.0
Group stage × 0

Knowledge beats luck — pick the right nation!
04
MORE FIGURES = MORE REWARDS
Each figure earns rewards independently. If you hold multiple figures from the same winning country, each one that lands in the TOP 10% earns its own share of the prize pool. A player with 3 Epic France figures wins 3× more than a player with 1.
05
3-LAYER REWARD SYSTEM
The prize pool is split 3 ways — every figure has a chance:
65% — TOP 10% GLOBALLY
Weight × WC Multiplier · Strongest figures from winning countries
25% — COUNTRY CHAMPION 🏆
Best figure of each country wins — 48 countries = 48 winners
Even if your country exits in the group stage!
10% — LEGENDARY JACKPOT ⚡
All Legendary figures split this pool equally — the rarest cards rewarded
06
CLAIM YOUR PRIZE
After the final on July 19, scores are calculated across all 3 layers. Claim directly to your wallet in CHZ — no intermediary, smart contract handles everything.
