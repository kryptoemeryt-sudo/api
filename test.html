QUICK GUIDE

This is not only mint and luck. Every scored position adds points, even before 11/11. A card can score, be sold, or be held to block other players.

01
SCORE
Mint or buy a card. When it enters your wallet, it scores for your Dream XI history.
02
SELL OR HOLD
After scoring, sell it for CHZ or hold it to block competitors from using the same card.
03
UPGRADE
Only your best scored card per position counts. A stronger card upgrades your squad.
04
TRADE SMART
Missing positions can be worth more than rarity. Check who needs your card before listing low.
KEY STRATEGY
Sell = profit now, but another player can score your card. Hold = protect your rank and control scarce positions.
SHOW REAL EXAMPLES
EXAMPLE 1: SCORE THEN SELLYou mint an Epic ST for 10 CHZ. It enters your wallet, scores for your Dream XI, then you can list it. Your score history stays saved.
EXAMPLE 2: HOLD TO BLOCKYou own the only strong LW on the market. If a rival needs LW to pass you, holding it can be stronger than selling cheap.
EXAMPLE 3: UPGRADE POSITIONYou already scored a 18 PTS RB. Later you buy a 36 PTS RB. Only the stronger RB counts, so your squad gains +18 PTS.
EXAMPLE 4: LAST MISSING CARDIf another player is 10/11 and your card completes his squad, that card may be worth far above floor. Check demand before listing.
EXAMPLE 5: NO NEED FOR 11/11You can compete with 3/11, 6/11 or 9/11. Each scored position adds points. A full 11/11 squad is stronger, but not required to enter the ranking.
